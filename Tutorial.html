Tutorial
MASTERM1ND is played using a standard mouse and keyboard.
The Passwords level involves clicking on the text field and entering a password attempt then clicking "Confirm"
The Phishing level involves multiple forms of mouse selections and then clicking the confirm button.
The Networks level combines play methods from the first 2 levels allowing you to select options and use text fields to complete it.
The Ports level allows you use the arrow keys and WASD to navigate a (VR Optional) 3D environment and also uses a text field as well.
